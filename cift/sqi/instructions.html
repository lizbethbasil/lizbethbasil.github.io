These guidelines to assess the quality/ freshness of shrimp by assessing the general quality attributes viz: appearance of shrimp outer surface, color, odour etc. in the demerit score. Each quality attributes has got sub quality attributes to assess the quality/ freshness of shrimp with different scale of measurements on each quality attributes. The description for checking/ evaluating the quality/ freshness of shrimp in terms of quality attributes is given below. Care must be taken to evaluate physically undamaged shrimp. Please avoid physically damaged shrimp from the lot when checking/ evaluating the quality/ freshness of shrimp.

The developed Shrimp Quality Index (SQI) ranges from 0 to 1, where 0 represents shrimp with excellent/ best quality and 1 represents shrimp with bad/ worst quality.

1 SHELL APPEARANCE Examine the surface of the shrimp and give appropriate score based on the visibility of surface.
2 SHELL MEAT ADHERENCE Examine the shrimp and give appropriate score based on the adherence of the meat to the shell.
3 MEAT APPEARANCE Examine the appearance of shrimp meat and give the appropriate score.
4 MEAT TEXTURE Examine the texture of shrimp meat and give appropriate score by pressing the meat gently.
5 MEAT COLOUR Examine the colour of shrimp meat and give the appropriate score.
6 ODOUR Smell the shrimp and give the appropriate score based on the odour.
